Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 250g digestive biscuits.
  • 125g butter in room temperature.
  • 600g soft cheese.
  • 60g caster sugar.
  • 200ml double cream.
  • handful strawberries or rasberries or any marmelade of your choice, to serve (optional)

Instructions

  • Crust: Mix crushed biscuits with melted butter, press into cake tin base, refrigerate
  • Filling: Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until smooth
  • Fold in whipped cream gently
  • Spread filling over crust, smooth the top
  • Chill in the fridge for 4-6 hours or overnight
  • Add fruit topping before serving, if desired
